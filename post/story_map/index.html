Story Map: Land Use and Land Cover Changes

May 2, 2020 1 min read

The story map "Cambios de Uso y Cobertura de la Tierra y Evolución de la Costa del Lago de Valencia en el Municipio Zamora del Estado Aragua, en el Período 1986-2014" (Shoreline evolution of Valencia lake and land use and land cover changes in Zamora municipality, Aragua state, Venezuela, period 1986-2014) was developed to take part in the National Award for Geographical Information System 2018 (NASIG), ESRI-Venezuela. This story map won the third prize for the best Story Map, more details here.
